Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu also spelled as tira mi su, translates to "lift me up"

This delicious treat is sure to make you feel better after a bad day.

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 8 spoons of sugar
  • vanilla essence
  • 500 grams of mascarpone
  • a pinch of salt
  • one pack of cocoa powder
  • 500 ml coffee or more
  • one pack of ladyfingers
  1. Separate the egg whites from the yolk
  2. Put the egg whites in a bowl
  3. throw a pinch of salt and mix well until the egg whites turn to foam
  4. throw 4 spoons of sugar in the mix and continue mixing until the 2 become a more solid foam
  5. In another bowl throw the yolks, 4 sugar spoons and the vanilla essence
  6. Continue mixing until the substance turns into a white/yellow creme
  7. Throw in the Mascarpone and mix slowly until the creme and mascarpone turn into one filling
  8. Dip the ladyfingers in the coffee, and then put them tightly packed one next to the other in a casserole
  9. Put the filling on top of the ladyfingers
  10. throw the cocoa powder on top of the filling
  11. repeat the last 3 steps again
  12. put the cassrole in the fridge for a few hours and then enjoy!
